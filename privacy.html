Privacy Policy

App: Little Explorers Puzzle

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Overview

Little Explorers Puzzle is a family-friendly puzzle game for kids. The app is designed to work offline and does not require users to create an account.

Data Collection

Little Explorers Puzzle does not collect personal information from users. The app does not use account registration, login, analytics, advertising networks, or online backend services.

Local Data

The app may store game progress, profile names, stars, settings, and similar gameplay information locally on the device. This information is used only to make the game work properly and to remember the player’s progress.

Local data stays on the device and is not sent to us.

Children’s Privacy

Little Explorers Puzzle is designed to be safe and simple for children and families. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Ads and Purchases

The app does not show ads and does not include active in-app purchases in this version.

Internet Connection

Little Explorers Puzzle does not require an internet connection for gameplay.

Third-Party Services

This version of the app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, login, or cloud backend services.
